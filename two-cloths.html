Two Cloths Shopify Store

Here is a store in Shopify I developed. For this store, we chose the Prestige Theme. Using themes allows you to get the most features for the best price. Once you confirm the theme that is best for you, I will write custom code to the theme to make it give you the features you need for your store. During the discovery process, I will learn what you need for your store and find some themes that give you as much of what you need as possible so you can decide on the theme you most want. Once we know your theme, I will custom develop the store and give you the store you want with the features you want and need.
The Prestige theme that includes the following features:

  • Store locator
  • Color swatches
  • Stock counter
  • In-menu promos
  • Mega menu
