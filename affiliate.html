Customizing your Website or Shopify Store

Themes are great and they can save you a bunch of money choosing a theme and then having me custom code features for your website or Shopify Store. In this video I go over what I did for a site to give it a feature that allows them to direct their shoppers to an affiliate website.

Deep Blue Spiral Galaxy Store

I am often asked to write custom code so our clients can have the featues they want at a price that is reasonable. We customize Shopify Stores, WordPress websites and of course completely custom websites. For the Blue Spiral Galaxy store, we added an affiliate link. They have a connection with another store and they now can direct their clients to that store with a link allowing their shoppers to purchase even more items. When a shopper clicks on the link and makes a purchase, they get a percentage of the sale. This can be a great way to get more business without adding extra inventory.
