TheWebDeveloper.dev

When working with TheWebDeveloper.dev you will work directly with the owner, Brent. Brent believes communication is the most important part of a business relationship and he has decades of experience working with clients. Brent's experience includes programming websites since the early 2000s, Knowledge & Technology Manager for a Big 4 accounting firm, and experience in sales and marketing as a licensed Realtor. Since Brent believes in communication so much, your first meeting will allow everyone to go over your needs and how Brent can help you get the website and store as you need. This also will allow you and Brent the opportunity to learn if Brent is the developer for you.
